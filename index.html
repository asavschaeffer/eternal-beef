Eternal Beef

The Eternal Beef Community Collective is dedicated to fostering healthy, creative, and supportive communities through skateboarding, cycling, arts education, and mutual aid.

We envision a Bay Area where every young person—regardless of financial background—has access to Skateboarding Instruction, Bike Programs, Safe Communal Spaces, Community Services, and Creative Expression.
