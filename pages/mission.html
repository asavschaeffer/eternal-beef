Vision

We envision a Bay Area where every young person—regardless of financial background—has access to:

  • Skateboarding Instruction
  • Creative Expression
  • Safe Communal Spaces
  • Transportation Empowerment
  • Community Belonging

Mission Statement

The Eternal Beef Community Collective is dedicated to fostering healthy, creative, and supportive communities through skateboarding, cycling, arts education, and mutual aid. We provide free or low-cost lessons, workshops, community service initiatives, and collaborative creative opportunities for youth, artists, and underserved residents.
